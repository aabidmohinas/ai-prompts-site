Best AI Prompt for YouTube Video Ideas

If you struggle to come up with YouTube video ideas, this AI prompt will generate high-quality, niche-specific ideas in seconds.

The Prompt

You are a YouTube content strategist.

My channel niche is: [INSERT NICHE]
My target audience is: [DESCRIBE AUDIENCE]
My goal is: [GROWTH / EDUCATION / ENTERTAINMENT]

Generate 20 YouTube video ideas that:
- Are highly clickable
- Solve real problems
- Match my niche
- Include emotional or curiosity-driven angles

Format the output as a numbered list.

How to Use This Prompt

  1. Replace the bracketed sections with your niche details.
  2. Paste the prompt into ChatGPT or another AI tool.
  3. Refine the best ideas into titles and scripts.

Who This Is For

  • New YouTubers
  • Faceless channel creators
  • Short-form and long-form creators

Bookmark this page and reuse the prompt whenever you need fresh ideas.
